# **Kickoff 2026 - Your Ultimate World Cup Companion**

**Kickoff 2026** is a mobile app designed for the 2026 FIFA World Cup, providing clear and up-to-date information about matches, teams, and results — all in one place.

---

## **🔍 Key Features**

- 📅 Full match schedule with group and knockout stages
- 🌐 Local time conversion for all match start times
- ⚽ View all qualified teams and their details
- 📊 Team history: FIFA ranking, previous World Cup records, and titles
- 🔔 Push notifications and ticket info (coming soon!)
